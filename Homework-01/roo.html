Roo

Roo is a cheerful and playful kangaroo joey who is the youngest member of the Hundred Acre Wood community. He is known for his boundless energy and eagerness to explore.

While Roo's character does not explicitly represent a mental health condition, his youthful exuberance and adventurous spirit can serve as a reminder of the importance of embracing joy and curiosity.

"Some people care too much. I think it's called Love.." - Roo
