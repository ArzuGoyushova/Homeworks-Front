Eeyore

Eeyore is a melancholic, gloomy donkey who is a part of Winnie the Pooh's circle of friends. Despite his perpetually pessimistic outlook on life, Eeyore is loved by his friends for his loyalty and sincerity.

Each of the main characters in Winnie the Pooh represents different aspects of mental health, and Eeyore symbolizes major depression disorder and low self-esteem.

Eeyore's interactions with his friends often highlight the importance of empathy, kindness, and supporting those who are struggling.

"A little consideration, a little thought for others, makes all the difference." - Eeyore
