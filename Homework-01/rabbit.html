Rabbit

Rabbit is a diligent and organized rabbit who takes great pride in maintaining order and structure in the Hundred Acre Wood. He is known for his meticulous attention to detail and his tendency to worry about everything being just right.

Rabbit's character has been associated with obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) in some interpretations, as he often exhibits behaviors such as arranging objects meticulously and becoming distressed when things are out of place.

"If you want to make it through this Pooh, you'd better keep up." - Rabbit
