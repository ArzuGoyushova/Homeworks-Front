Tigger

Tigger is an energetic and boisterous tiger who loves to bounce and is always full of enthusiasm. He is known for his distinctive personality and his catchphrase "T-T-F-N: Ta-ta for now!"

Each of the main characters in Winnie the Pooh represents different aspects of mental health, and Tigger's character has been associated with attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) in some interpretations.

Tigger's hyperactivity, impulsivity, and tendency to constantly seek new experiences might be interpreted as manifestations of his condition.

"The most wonderful thing about Tiggers is you're the only one!" - Tigger
