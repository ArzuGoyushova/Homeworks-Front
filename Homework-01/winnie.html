Winnie the Pooh

Winnie the Pooh, also known as Pooh Bear, is a lovable, honey-obsessed bear who lives in the Hundred Acre Wood. He is known for his simple-minded yet wise outlook on life.

Each of the main characters in Winnie the Pooh represents different aspects of mental health, and Pooh himself symbolizes struggles with food and eating habits, often associated with an eating disorder.

Pooh's adventures teach valuable lessons about friendship, kindness, and the importance of embracing life's simple pleasures.

"Sometimes the smallest things take up the most room in your heart." - Winnie the Pooh
