Owl

Owl is a wise and knowledgeable owl who serves as a mentor and advisor to the other characters in the Hundred Acre Wood. He is known for his intelligence, eloquence, and love of storytelling.

Some interpretations of Owl's character suggest traits associated with narcissistic personality disorder (NPD), such as a grandiose sense of self-importance and a need for admiration. However, Owl's portrayal can also be seen as a reflection of his confidence and leadership qualities.

"Well, it's like this, little Piglet. We all have to go to school, and study and learn our lessons. And there's always something to be learned." - Owl
