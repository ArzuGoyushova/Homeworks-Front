Piglet

Piglet is a small, timid pig who is a close friend of Winnie the Pooh. Despite his small size and nervous disposition, Piglet often shows great courage when faced with challenges.

Each of the main characters in Winnie the Pooh represents different aspects of mental health, and Piglet symbolizes anxiety and insecurity.

Piglet's adventures with his friends in the Hundred Acre Wood highlight the importance of bravery, friendship, and overcoming fears.

"The things that make me different are the things that make me." - Piglet
